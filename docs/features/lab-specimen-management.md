# Lab Specimen Management

**Status:** Draft
**Branch:** `feature/lab-specimen-management`
**Date:** 2026-02-18

## Overview

This feature adds lab specimen tracking and management capabilities to the PMS, enabling clinicians to order lab tests, track specimen collection and processing, and view results within patient records.

- Specimen labeling and tracking
